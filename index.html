How to Use

1
Pick a level -- start with N5 or 10級 if you're new. Each level teaches you kanji through spaced repetition.
2
Quiz mode -- answer reading, meaning, and compound questions. Get a kanji right and it advances through SRS levels.
3
Watch the colors shift -- kanji start gray (unseen), turn red (apprentice), orange, gold, then green (mastered) as you learn.
4
Come back when due -- the SRS system schedules reviews at increasing intervals: 4hr, 1d, 3d, 1w, 2w. Due kanji glow for attention.
5
Use Blink mode -- rapid full-screen flashcards that auto-advance. Perfect for quick review sessions on the go.
6
Unlock post-mastery -- master all kanji in a level to unlock Confusion Pairs, Compound Mastery, and Sentence Context challenges.
